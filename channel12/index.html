Invest in your hair. It's the crown on your head you never take off.

Channel 12 is a hair salon-cafe hybrid that specializes in dyeing hair in quirky and bold colors. Whether you're looking for a snip, a perm, highlights, or extensions, you can always count on us to satisfy your hunger by relaxing in our cafe. By practicing sustainable living through the use of plant-based and enrivonmental-friendly products, our motto is to be kind to the planet—while having great hair.

It's not just a hair color, it's a state of mind.

Why Channel 12?

Affordable hair care and service without any unnecessary costs

Our services cost 60% less than the average upscale hair salon. Channel 12 has innovated hair care to tailor to your budget.

Plant-based and cruelty-free products

In addition to being affordable, our hair care products are made of plant-based ingredients, and come in environmentally-friendly packages. We practice safe and sustainable service.


We serve you food and drinks while you get your hair done

As one of the few hair salon-cafe hybrids in the country, kick back at our cafe to fill your stomach. From milkshakes to cream pies to bacon and eggs, you won't need to leave Channel 12 to get food.

Bring home a personalized DIY hair coloring kit

You can opt to buy a home do-it-yourself hair coloring kit to tone, bleach and dye your hair in circumstances that you can't pay us a visit. Placing an order is only a few clicks away, with delivery fees shouldered by us.
